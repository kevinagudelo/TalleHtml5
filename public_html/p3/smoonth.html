Smooth in colors

The float color consists of a glass sheet made by floating the molten glass over a layer of molten tin. This method provides the glass with a uniform thickness and a very flat surface, making it the most used glass in the construction. It is also called flat glass, however not all flat glass are glass made by the flotation system.
